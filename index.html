Questions

What do I need to bring to my driving lessons?

You need your provisional licence and comfortable shoes. Optional: water and a notepad.

How long is each lesson?

Each driving lesson lasts 1 hour as standard. You can also book double lessons (2 hours) if you prefer extended practice time.

Can I use my own car for lessons?

Yes, you may use your own car if it's insured for learner use and road legal. However, we recommend using the instructor's car for better safety and dual controls.

What happens if I need to cancel a lesson?

Please give at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. Short-notice cancellations may result in a charge.

How do I book or reschedule a lesson?

You can book or reschedule a lesson by sending a WhatsApp message or calling directly. A booking system will be added soon for convenience.
